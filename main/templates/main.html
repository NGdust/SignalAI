Welcome to SignalAI Prototype.

This is a prototype of a product we're developing called Reflection. We chose for purposes of this protoype the topic of relationships, because we all struggle with relationships in our lives. So think of a relationship in your life, preferrably one that is current, or if don't have a current one then think of someone from the past and identify their firstname and their relationship to you.

At the end of the session you will receive XXX

Reflection is an augmented human intelligence App that has the ability to bypass the natural biases of our minds. From what people say, to what people think they say, Reflection is able to give us an insight into our underlying thoughts, emotions and behaviors that aren't always apparent to us.

Tutorial:

1. Our main goal is to detect your emotional state

2. If you want to increase the level of of emotions detection accuracy, please, make the answers as informative as possible (e.g. more than 100 symbols).

3. We process data through AI applications. One of the apps detects emotions and the other one proceeds with semantic analysis.

4. After each answer we display 3 dominant emotions based on your answers analysis.
